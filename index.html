Instrunctions

  1. You will be given a random goal number at the start of the game.
  2. There are four colored stones to the right. (Blue, Green, Red, Turqoise)
  3. By clicking on a stone you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  4. You win the game by matching your total score to the goal number.
  5. You lose the game if your total goes above the goal number.
  6. The value of each stone is hidden from you until you click on it.
  7. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each stone.
